Manakrishi

When Drones Speak The Language of Farms

The Problem

Traditional farming in India faces significant challenges: health hazards from manual pesticide spraying, inefficient resource usage leading to higher costs, and a lack of real-time data for crop monitoring. Farmers often struggle with labor shortages and inconsistent crop yields.

The Solution

Manakrishi revolutionizes agriculture by bringing precision drone spraying services to every farmer's doorstep. By combining advanced drone technology with expert agronomy, we ensure micro-doses of nutrients and pesticides are deployed exactly where needed, reducing waste and ensuring safety.

Key Technologies

A fusion of hardware and software to create a seamless ecosystem for farmers.

Drone Technology IoT Sensors React Native (App) Firebase Data Analytics

Impact & Vision

  • 100% Health Safety: Removes human exposure to harmful chemicals.
  • Cost Reduction: Optimized spraying reduces input costs for farmers.
  • Sustainability: Minimizes water and pesticide usage through precision application.
  • Empowerment: Making smart farming tools accessible to rural farmers.
