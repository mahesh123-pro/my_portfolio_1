Prolance Case Study

Prolance – Professional Networking & Career Growth Platform

The ultimate platform for students, professionals, and recruiters to discover opportunities, collaborate, and master new skills.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Deployment: Vercel

Key Features

  • Job and Internship discovery tailored to skills
  • Tech events, hackathons, and workshops
  • Community discussions and knowledge sharing
  • Curated learning resources and roadmaps

System Architecture

A modern full-stack application leveraging Next.js for server-side rendering and static site generation, ensuring fast load times and optimal SEO. The backend is powered by Node.js, storing dynamic community and job data in MongoDB for flexibility and scalability.

Impact / Metrics

  • A centralized hub replacing fragmented tools for students and recruiters
  • Streamlined the talent discovery and hiring process

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Managing high volumes of community discussions and real-time job postings.
Solution: Implemented efficient pagination and state management, utilizing Next.js API routes with MongoDB to optimize data fetching and rendering.
