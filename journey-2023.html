The Academic Foundation

2023 marked the beginning of my formal journey into Engineering. Entering **MLRITM** as an ECE student was a significant milestone. It wasn't just about passing exams; it was about building a deep understanding of how systems—both electronic and digital—function at their core.

While my heart was already in software, the ECE curriculum introduced me to the world of **hardware, circuits, and signal processing**. This dual perspective became my unique strength. I started seeing how code interacts with the physical world through microcontrollers and communication protocols. Beyond the classroom, I became active in the college technical community, participating in workshops and starting to collaborate with like-minded peers who shared my passion for innovation.
